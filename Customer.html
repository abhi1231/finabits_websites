Customer Retention

Customer retention refers to the strategies and actions a business employs to keep existing customers engaged and loyal over the long term. It is a critical aspect of business growth, as retaining customers typically costs less than acquiring new ones and contributes to consistent revenue. Here's an in-depth look at customer retention:


Importance of Customer Retention:

Cost Efficiency: Retaining an existing customer is 5-25 times cheaper than acquiring a new one

Revenue Growth: Existing customers are more likely to make repeat purchases and try new products.

Brand Advocacy: Satisfied customers are more likely to recommend your brand to others, acting as ambassadors..

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Loyal customers tend to generate more value over time through repeat business and referrals.
